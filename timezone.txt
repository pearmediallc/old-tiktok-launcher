bout time zones
Last updated: May 2025
When you create an account on TikTok Ads Manager, you'll be able to select your time zone. TikTok Ads Manager works with time zones that are either full hour or half hour ahead or behind Greenwich Mean Time (GMT)/Coordinated Universal Time (UTC).

Note: TikTok Ads Manager does not support time zones that use quarter hour deviations and daylight savings time.

Features
Your time zone affects:

The time your data is displayed on the platform, including in reports.

Your ad delivery settings, including start and end times.

Your bill cycle dates for invoicing when calculating your total ad costs.

Note: You will be unable to change your time zone after selecting it during account registration.

Find your selected time zone
Use the following steps to access your selected time zone in Tiktok Ads Manager.

Log in to TikTok Ads Manager.

Go to the Tools tab.

Click Account setup and view your selected time zone.

Time Zone Converter
The Time Zone Converter in TikTok Ads Manager helps you manage campaign start times, end times, and dayparting schedules without relying on manual calculations or third-party tools. As of March 7, 2025, the Time Zone Converter features:

Local time scheduling: You can schedule ads based on your local time zone or your target market's time zone.

Automatic time conversion: The selected time is automatically converted to the Business Center account time zone for consistency in reporting, ad delivery, and budgeting.

Daylight Saving Time adjustments: The system accounts for regions that observe daylight saving time and adjusts accordingly.

If a region observes Daylight Savings Time, you can select this option from a dropdown list.

If the start and end times fall under different time zones due to the duration of the campaign, you may need to adjust the end time by one hour forward or backward to ensure accurate delivery.

You will be informed by a pop-up notification if the start and end times fall under different time zones, such as during daylight saving transitions.

Improved time zone display: The dropdown menu now displays both the UTC format and the time zone name (e.g., Eastern Standard Time).




About Timezone Converter in TikTok Ads Manager
Last updated: June 2025
When you schedule an ad campaign, it can be challenging to deal with misalignment between account timezones, target market timezones, and Daylight Savings Time. The Timezone Converter in TikTok Ads Manager provides an easier and more accurate way to schedule campaigns across different regions.

Features
The Timezone Converter in TikTok Ads Manager offers:

Local timezone scheduling: You can schedule ads based on their local timezone or the timezone of the target market by selecting the desired option from a dropdown menu.

Automatic conversion: The scheduled time will automatically be converted to the user's Business Center account timezone. This conversion is applicable for the following types of scheduling: selecting start time only, selecting start time and end time, and selecting start time and duration.

Dayparting: You can set updayparting based on your selected timezone, and the system will automatically convert it to the Business Center account timezone for delivery.

Timezone display: The dropdown menu displays both the Universal timecode (UTC) format and the timezone name to eliminate confusion and reduce the need for manual checks.

Multiple timezones: You will be informed by a pop-up notification if the start and end times fall under different timezones, such as during daylight saving transitions. If a region observes Daylight Savings Time, the converter will account for the shift, so you don't need to make manual corrections. If the start and end times fall under different timezones due to the duration of the campaign, you may need to adjust the end time by 1 hour forward or backward to ensure accurate delivery.

Schedule Editing: Scheduled start and end times can be edited as long as they haven't passed.

Conditions for the schedule editing feature for Timezone Converter in TikTok Ads Manager
If both the start and end times are in the future, you can edit the timezone, start time, and end time.

If the start time has passed, but the end time is still in the future, you can edit the end time, but the timezone and start time remain locked.

If both the start and end times have passed, no edits can be made.
