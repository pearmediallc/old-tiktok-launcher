Create Smart+ Lead Generation Campaigns
This article walks you through the steps to create Smart+ Lead Generation Campaigns.

Based on your preference for the optimization location, you can create a Smart+ Lead Generation Campaign with optimization location as Instant Form or Website. For detailed instructions on creating each type, refer to their respective sections: "Create a Smart+ Lead Generation Campaign with optimization location as Instant Form" and "Create a Smart+ Lead Generation Campaign with optimization location as Website".

Create a Smart+ Lead Generation Campaign with optimization location as Instant Form
Prerequisites
Ensure that you have an existing Instant Form within your ad account.

To create an Instant Form, use Instant Page Editor SDK and set businessType to 1. Then call /page/get/ to get the page ID of the Instant Form through page_id.
